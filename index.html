About Me

Veronika is a software developer from Russia who currently lives in New Jersey. She loves to travel, read books, and act in theatre. In her spare time, she likes to do areal yoga, read tarot cards, cook vegeratian food, and develop apps to track health and fitness for her two guinea pigs, Bo & Peter. Her big aspiration is to create software that helps lots of people!
